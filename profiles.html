curated profiles

You download Instagram. You put in your email, type in a password, choose a username. You're now prompted to arguably the most important part of creating an account, the bio. For many people, typing out the most interesting bio is one of the key aspects of creating a profile.

Creating the short and cheeky bio for Twitter, Instagram, and all the other social media platforms is essential to many because it's essentially like creating a tag line for your own brand to sell yourself to others.

how profiles are curated

There are numerous aspects of social media where people curate their profiles in. In curating their profiles, they don't realize that they are slowly curating this virtual version of themselves that isn't necessarily the real self. These are some of the ways that people curate themselves online.

the impact

Curated identities could potentially be harmful to impressionable users, particularly those of a young age as it encourages behavior that only accepts and highlights the "good" or what society deems as "ideal" parts of oneself. People are encouraged to fit the status quo and mold themselves into a person that someone else would be more inclined to follow, swipe right on, or even hire.
